Our Mission Statement

We are a 6 man team out from New Jersey, United States, and we have the sole purpose of spreading our love for medicine with the rest of the world! However, we have noticed that CTE (chronic traumatic Encephalopathy) has gone under the radar and has hidden itself among our most popular sports: football, soccer, combat sports, and so many more. We have dedicated this organization to raise awarness and bring change to this current issue. We hope that we can make the next leaders of America safe and knowlegable regarding CTE as a whole. As of now we have gone to HOSA SLC 2024 and are competing in HOSA ILC 2024! We thank all of you for your support to make this dream of ours come true.
